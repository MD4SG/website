MD4SG 2023

MD4SG Workshop at IJCAI 2023

August 19-21, 2023

Call for contributions

We invite submissions to the Mechanism Design for Social Good (MD4SG) workshop at IJCAI 2023, which will be held in Cape Town, South Africa, on 19, 20, or 21 August 2023. The workshop will be for one day and the final date is to be determined by the IJCAI organizers.

 

The deadline for submissions is 26 April 2023.

The workshop aims to highlight work where techniques from algorithms, optimization, and mechanism design, along with insights from other disciplines, can improve access to opportunity for historically underserved and marginalized communities. We value work that bridges research and practice, such as through collaborations with government and non-profits or deployment to end-users.

We encourage submissions that have been inspired by African contexts or have been conducted in Africa. Applications of interest include:

  • agricultural markets,
  • conservation,
  • healthcare,
  • education,
  • microfinance,
  • natural resource management, and
  • youth unemployment.

We also encourage two-page research pitches, particularly from practitioners, highlighting relevant problems, datasets, or opportunities for research collaboration.

The programs at past MD4SG workshops (including at FAccT, EC, and WINE) and the EAAMO conference provide examples of the type of research we will feature at this workshop.

Important Information

Submission deadline: 26 April 2023, Anywhere on Earth (AoE) timezone

Link to submit papers: cmt3.research.microsoft.com/MD4SG2023

The workshop is in-person only. At least one of the authors of accepted papers must attend in-person in Cape Town.

Papers may only be submitted to one IJCAI-23 workshop.

If you have any questions, please email organizers@md4sg.com
