Meetup @ NeurIPS 2021

December 3-14, Virtual

NeurIPS 2021, curated by MD4SG

Welcome to NeurIPS, MD4SG-style! The schedule for this conference is quite large, so we've curated a list of programming relevant to the MD4SG community, whether by speaker/organizer/author or topic.

We will have an organized MD4SG meetup time on December 13 on the MD4SG Discord on the #neurips2021 channel. The channel will be open throughout the conference — we encourage you all to visit and discuss throughout the conference.

Note that all times are in Pacific Standard Time (PST) as is the timezone used by the NeurIPS conference. (Convert to your timezone.) See the full NeurIPS 2021 schedule.

This MD4SG meetup at NeurIPS 2021 is organized by Jessie Finocchiaro, Corinna Hertweck, and Lily Xu from the MD4SG Community Engagement team.

Many of the events and talks below require a NeurIPS registration to access. All participants have read the NeurIPS 2021 Code of Conduct and agree to abide by it.
